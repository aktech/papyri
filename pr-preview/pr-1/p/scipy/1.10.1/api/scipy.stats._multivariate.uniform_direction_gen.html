This generates one random direction, a vector on the surface of :math:`S^2`. Alternatively, the object may be called (as a function) to return a frozen distribution with fixed `dim` parameter. Here, we create a `uniform_direction` with ``dim=3`` and draw 5 observations. The samples are then arranged in an array of shape 5x3.
